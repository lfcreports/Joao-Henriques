About Us


     L.F.C. Reports is a non-official football magazine founded in 2023 which publishes monthly articles about Liverpool Football Club. With an emphasis on a tactical perspective, we provide in-depth analysis on 'The Reds' recent perfomances, season and transfer market.
     What are the standout performers the recent month? How was Liverpool F.C. season? Which signings might produce a huge and positive impact for the club? Read the single-authored articles below.
     There is also an option to see other analysis on players from different clubs, which demonstrates that, with a wide range of content, L.F.C. Reports is where you find the information to follow Liverpool Football Club and develop a better understanding of the beautiful game.
