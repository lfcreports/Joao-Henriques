About Us




     Decoding Football is an amateur football magazine founded in 2023 which publishes monthly articles about various male players in Europe's best leagues.

     With an emphasis on the tactical perspective, we provide in-depth analysis on the data of a certain footballer and whether or not he performed or will perform well.

     You can quickly access the articles below and develop a better understanding of the beautiful game!
